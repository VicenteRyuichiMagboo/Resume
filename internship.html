Internship Learning


My internship was truly a different experience than anything that I’ve encountered in my lifetime. Being surrounded by people who were in your position before and finally being able to hone their skills is something that I valued during my internship. Being taught data analysis through the use of meltwater and UX to be applied in a company portfolio website by my mentors was eye-opening. To use my weakness of overthinking in the field of UX, and to use my strength for analyzing to see things that most people and give them insights. Because of this, my passion for learning more has bloomed once more. Excited to learn more about myself, and to exceed the expectations that I’ve set for myself in the future. I would like to thank my internship for teaching me that.
